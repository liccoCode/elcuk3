5.0 out of 5 stars Very handy piece of device, 11 July 2012
By 
sano - See all my reviews
Amazon Verified Purchase(What is this?)
This review is from: EasyAcc 8400mAh Portable Emergency Universal USB external/extended battery pack and charger with Dual USB output (Electronics)
Excellent portable charger that charges my phone in emergencies when i need it the most.
The external charger is a very sturdy device which does weigh slightly more than i expected but this is not much of an issue as u get use to it. The charger itself has four LED lights which indicates the amount of power left in the device. 2 USB ports ( one of them a 0.6A and the other a 2.0A which charges your phone/ desired device faster). It has 1 mini usb port which use to charge up the external charger itself through an AC outlet. The charger itself is roughly the size of a pack of cards.

I would say the portable charger charges my samsung galaxy s2 about 3-4 times before it dies. However i only use it in emergencies when my phone battery is about to die when im out and charging through an AC output is not possible so it can last for a few weeks between charges easily. It really depends on how u use it. Also dont be alarmed if on the 1st charge cycle your external charger loses battery quickly as this gets better in time as you give it a few more charges. I would suggest to let it charge 1st time overnight b4 using it to charge any of your devices.

Along with the charger included are two USB cables of different sizes which u connect into the external charger and the other side of the cable is a port which fits several small pieces included. I generally use the micro usb piece of which they include two of which is quite helpful as i can use 1 cable and 1mini usb piece primarily for charging the portable charger through an AC output and the other cable and mini usb for my phone so i dont need to switch bits and bobs about. There are also few more pieces which u can attach onto the cable to charge different devices. I have tried an old nokia with the bigger circle connector and a nokia e6 which has the smaller circle connector and both charges perfectly fine. There is also an piece to charge apple devices and i've tried this on my girlfriends iphone and ipod nano and its charges fine. I was pleasantly surprised when a piece connected into my bluetooth ps3 controller and started to charge, which was a great addition. However, there wasn't a piece to fit for my nintendo 3ds( which i hoped as it said it charges nintendo ds so i was hopeful it charged the 3ds also but this wasnt to be) Nevertheless a great piece of kit.

the only downside was that it did not include an UK adapter, which they did mention but i still thought they shoud provide this for immediate use. However as i knew about this i bought an EU to UK adapter on amazon along with this so they got dispatched the same time. I would suggest buying an EU TO UK adaptor at the same time as the easyacc8400maH portable charger.
The adapter i bought was for 1.04(prices may change overtime) which can be found here:
http://www.amazon.co.uk/Travel-Adaptor-Plug-Worldwide-European/dp/B000P0FIUM/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1341971675&sr=8-1
and it works perfectly fine with the external charger.
If u have a 3pin shaver adapter that also works as i tried this out of curiosity when i received the product.

All in all, i would give this product 10/10 and highly recommend it to everyone.

update 27/07/12 (Customer service review): Had a slight problem with the product and easyacc store was quick to respond and despatch me a new one free of charge. They were very helpful. One of the best customer services i have received.

5.0 out of 5 stars very useful accessory, 22 May 2012
By 
nadget - See all my reviews
Amazon Verified Purchase(What is this?)
This review is from: EasyAcc 8400mAh Portable Emergency Universal USB external/extended battery pack and charger with Dual USB output (Electronics)
After using a similar Duracel product for a year, I wanted a similar unit that was very light and compact but with 4x the capacity. I use this product to charge my Nokia E51 phone and my kodak Zx5 playcam while I am out and about. The capacity enables me to use my digital equipment all day. Actually the charger is so good it could most likely last for 2-3 days charging my devices before it needs topping up. The EasyAcc is extremely light (185g on the scales) and comes with a whole variety of cables to use on many different devices. It charges via a USB inlet (cable provided) and comes with a mains to USB charging plug. However this is a european plug. You need a euro to UK mains converter plug to use the provided plug in the UK. You dont need to use the provided plug. I use the mains USB charger that came with the Kodak playcam. Alternatively, to charge it up, you can use the USB port on a laptop or PC or the charger that comes with an iPod. It doesnt come with a 5V car charger which some people would have found useful. There are 4 blue LEDs which indicate the state of charge. These are activated when the unit is powered on or is being charged. The unit automatically powers off if when not in use. The only features I would have liked would be a shockproof and waterproof casing, but for the price I think this an excellent product and would thoroughly recommend it.
